The S.E.A. Aquarium on Sentosa Island is one of the world’s largest aquariums, housing over 100,000 marine animals across 1,000 species. Located in Resorts World Sentosa, it features 49 habitats, including shark tunnels and dolphin lagoons. Highlights include hammerhead sharks, bottlenose dolphins, and Japanese spider crabs. The aquarium is ideal for families and marine life enthusiasts. Interactive programs like dolphin encounters are available. The Ocean Restaurant offers dining with underwater views. The aquarium promotes marine conservation through educational displays. It’s accessible via Sentosa Express or cable car. Visitors can spend 2–3 hours exploring. The aquarium is a must-visit for its immersive marine experience. Tickets include access to all exhibits. Photography is allowed, but flash is prohibited. The aquarium is part of Sentosa’s family-friendly attractions. Nearby dining options cater to various tastes. The facility is well-maintained and engaging.  
**Location**: 8 Sentosa Gateway, Singapore 098269  
**Timings**: 10:00 AM–5:00 PM  
**Entry Fee**: S$43 (adults), S$32 (children)  
**Tip**: Visit early to avoid school group crowds.
